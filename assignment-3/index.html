Homemade Roasted Pumpkin Seeds

A traditional tasty fall snack

Prep:15 Min / Total:40 Min / Servings:12

Ingredients

  • 1 small (6lb) fresh-well-ripened pumpkin
  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350o.F. Cut pumpkin in half. Remove the membrane and seeds.
    Remove most of the pulp from the seeds(leave some pulp on-it adds to the flavor;
    for some reason, do not rinse the seeds    ).
  2. Spread seeds in a shallow, jelly roll pan with sides, 15x10x 1 1/2 inches.
    Spray seeds with cooking spray; sprinkle with salt.
  3. Bake 25 to 30 mineutes or until browned; cool.
